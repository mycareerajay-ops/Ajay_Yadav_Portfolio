Featured Projects

Ramboll CAD Menus

Role: Digital Product Lead

The Problem

Inefficient workflows and significant compliance risks caused by non-standardized CAD tools across the organization.

My Solution

I initiated the project by securing executive buy-in with a data-driven business case. We then built a standardized C# toolset (CAD Menus) with a central configurator to enforce project standards and centralize tools for over 2,500 engineers.

The Outcome

Successfully delivered a 15% efficiency gain, saving an estimated 22,185 hours annually for a single business unit.

Cab-Connect: Ride Comparison App (Personal)

Role: Product Manager

The Problem

Users waste significant time and money by having to manually open and compare prices and wait times across multiple ride-hailing apps.

My Solution

I conducted user research and interviews to validate the user problem. From there, I designed the end-to-end user experience, creating user journey maps, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes, and defined a prioritized backlog.

The Outcome

Produced a complete, clickable prototype, a full product specification, and a go-to-market growth strategy built around a North Star Metric & AARRR framework.
